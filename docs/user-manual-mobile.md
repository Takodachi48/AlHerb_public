**AlgoHerbarium Mobile User Manual**

**End-to-End Mobile Flow**
1. Complete onboarding (`/onboarding`) and choose sign‑in or registration (`/auth/choice`).
2. Verify your email if prompted (`/auth/verify-email`).
3. Arrive on the Home tab and complete profile details for personalization.
4. Use the tabs to explore herbs, identify plants, check safety, and join the community.
5. Save favorites and remedies so you can return later.

**Home → Your Launchpad**
1. Greeting and account snapshot appear at the top.
2. Quick tools route to Symptoms (recommendations), Safety, and Blog posting.
3. “Resume activity” opens your latest scan or recommendation.
4. Community blogs and daily insights appear when available.
5. The floating Chatbot button is always available on Home.

**Find Herbs → Learn Details**
1. Open the Herbs tab and search by name or scientific name.
2. Apply category chips to narrow results.
3. Tap a herb to open the detail screen.
4. Review overview, uses, preparation, dosage, and safety warnings.
5. Save to favorites, share, or jump to “Find Nearby.”

**Explore the Map**
1. Open the Herb Map tab for location-based discovery.
2. Filter by herb and location type.
3. Enable device location for nearby search.
4. Tap markers for details and context.

**Identify a Plant**
1. Open Image Processing and upload a clear plant photo.
2. Review confidence and alternative matches.
3. Use scan history to revisit past results.

**Get Recommendations**
1. Follow the three‑step flow: Symptoms → Profile → Results.
2. Choose “For me” or “For someone else.”
3. Provide age group, gender, and health context.
4. Review ranked herb recommendations and open remedy details.
5. Save remedies for later reference.

**Review Safety**
1. Open Safety & Interactions (`/screens/interactions`).
2. Check herb interactions and contraindications.
3. Review combination warnings when relevant.

**Join the Community**
1. Open Community to read blog posts.
2. Create or edit a blog (`/community/create-blog`).
3. Open blog details for full content.

**Manage Profile and History**
1. Profile and Edit Profile update your account details.
2. Favorites lists saved herbs.
3. Saved Remedies stores bookmarked recommendations.
4. Scan History and Recommendation History track past activity.

**Notifications, Help, and Chatbot**
1. Notifications show app updates and alerts.
2. Help & FAQ, Privacy & Security, and Send Feedback provide support.
3. Chatbot (`/chatbot`) answers questions and gives guidance.

**Troubleshooting Flow**
1. No results? Clear filters and broaden search terms.
2. Recommendations blocked? Review safety warnings before retrying.
3. Map not working? Enable device location permissions.
4. Identification uncertain? Upload a clearer photo.
